What is KaemKaar

Kaem Kaar is a mobile-first marketplace connecting workers and hirers across J&K — eliminating middlemen and enabling people to earn on their own terms.

The Problem

J&K has one of India's highest educated unemployment rates. Yet households struggle to find reliable help, businesses overpay, and skilled workers lose income daily due to lack of digital infrastructure.

Our Solution

Kaem Kaar transforms Kashmir’s informal job system into a transparent, fast, and merit-based digital marketplace.
