What is KaemKaar

Kaem Kaar is a mobile-first marketplace connecting workers and hirers across J&K. It eliminates middlemen, formalizes informal work, and creates fair income opportunities.

  • No reliable platform to connect workers and employers
  • Middlemen take up to 30% of wages
  • Workers lose income due to no visibility
  • Educated youth lack local earning opportunities

The Problem

Kashmir has one of India's highest educated unemployment rates. Despite demand, there is no digital infrastructure connecting work to workers efficiently.

Our Solution

Kaem Kaar transforms the informal economy into a transparent, digital, and merit-based system.
