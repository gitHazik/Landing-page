What is KaemKaar

Connecting Communities Through Work

"Kaam Karo, Aage Badho."
Work. Grow. Thrive.

Kaem Kaar is a mobile-first marketplace connecting workers and hirers across J&K — eliminating middlemen, formalizing the informal economy, and creating fair, flexible income opportunities.

  • No digital platform exists to connect workers and hirers directly.
  • Middlemen take up to 30% of daily wages.
  • Skilled workers lose income due to lack of visibility.
  • Educated youth have skills but no local earning opportunities.

The Problem

Kashmir's Employment Crisis Has No Infrastructure

J&K has one of India's highest educated unemployment rates. Households struggle to find reliable help, businesses overpay, and skilled workers lose income daily due to inefficient word-of-mouth systems and middlemen.
