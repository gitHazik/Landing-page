What is KaemKaar

Connecting Communities Through Work

"Kaam Karo, Aage Badho."
Work. Grow. Thrive.

Kaem Kaar is a mobile-first marketplace connecting workers and hirers directly across J&K.

  • No digital platform exists for local J&K workers.
  • Middlemen take up to 30% of daily wages.
  • Skilled workers lose daily earnings due to lack of visibility.
